This mod adds a large amount of enemies, a hefty amount of pets, and a few other things. For a list of all content in this mod, please visit the homepage. For a brief list of what we offer:

Enemies:
 * 66 Common-ish enemies
 * 2 Bosses

Items:
* 38 Accessories
 * 1 Accessory (Consolation Prize, see homepage)
 * 2 Ammo Types
 * 1 Armor Set (3 pieces)
 * 3 Armor Pieces (Vanity)
 * 1 Furniture
 * 52 Pets (Many of which have alt skins)
 * 54 Pet Vanity (For Cute Slimes)
 * 1 Pet Vanity (For many other pets)
 * 2 Tools
 * 11 Weapons

Fun:
 * A lot, hopefully!
