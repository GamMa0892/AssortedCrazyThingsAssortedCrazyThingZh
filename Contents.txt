BOSSES
============
Foldfish
	- A silly boss made when both devs joked over a typo
	- Animated Tome enemies can drop an Origami Manual, which will summon the Foldfish
		- Using the tome also spawns a bunch of harmless Foldfish Babies
	- The boss fights similarly to the King Slime, though its summoned minions are useless and harmless
	- Purely intended as a joke boss, not a proper one
	- Drops: Origami Hat, Origami Crane
(BOSS NAME HERE)
	- Unassuming and invincible bird that walks around the Dungeon after Skeletron is defeated
	- In its presence, souls will burst out of defeated enemies
	- (BOSS NAME HERE) will seek out and eat these souls, growing bigger as it consumes them
	- Use a net to slow down (BOSS NAME HERE)'s feast, though it will throw bones at you if you try this when it's eating
	- After eating a few souls, it grows larger and more menacing
	- If it gorges itself even more, it will become a mass of flying bone and moss
	- It will attack by extending its talons while flying above the player
	- Once killed, it will not naturally spawn anymore
	- Craft or find an Idol of Decay to summon it again at any time in the Dungeon
	- Drops: Bones, Dessicated Leather, Freed Dungeon Soul, (BOSS NAME HERE) Mask

ACCESSORIES
============
Anomalous Wings
	- drops from Giant Anomalocaris.
	- Allows slowfall, and allows quick travel in water
Bipolar Candle in a Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Water Candle in a Balloon x1 + Peace Candle in a Balloon x1
	- It does exactly what you'd expect it to, except it doesn't
Bottled Dreams
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Bottle x1 + Pixie Dust x10
	- Allows you to double jump. Can not be used in tandem with Blessed Apple's double jump
Everburning Candelabra
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Everburning Candle + Everburning Cursed Candle + Everburning Shadow Candle + Everfrozen Candle
	- Combines the effect of the items used to craft it
Everburning Candle
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Magma Stone x1 + Soul of Light x50
	- Like the Magma Stone, but from every source of player damage, including summons
Everburning Cursed Candle
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Magma Stone x1 + Cursed Flame x50
	- Like the Everburning Candle, but causes the Cursed Flames debuff
Everburning Shadow Candle
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Magma Stone x1 + Soul of Night x50
	- Like the Everburning Candle, but causes the Shadowflame debuff. Shorter duration than the other candles
Everfrozen Candle
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Magma Stone x1 + Ice Block x50
	- Like the Everburning Candle, but causes the Frostburn debuff
Festive Mirror
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Hand Warmers x1 + Pocket Mirror x1
	- Provides immunity to the Chilled, Frozen, and Stoned debuffs
Loot Magnet
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Greedy Ring x1 + Celestial Magnet x1 + Heartreach Potion x1
	- Increases pickup range on coins, hearts, and mana, lowers shop prices, and causes coins to sometimes drop from enemies
Massive Bundle of Balloons
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Bundle of Balloons x1 + Honey Balloon x1 + Fart in a Balloon x1 + Sharkron Balloon x1 + Lucky Horseshoe x1 + Bottled Dreams x1
	- Gives you six extra jumps, negates fall damage, and increases jump height
Peace Candle in a Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Peace Candle x1 + Shiny Red Balloon x1
	- Reduced Enemy Spawn Rate, Increased Jump Height, and very faintly glows in the dark
Sigil of Last Stand
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Sigil of Pain Suppression x1 + Sigil of Retreat x1
	- Teleports you to your spawn point and drastically increases your defense for a brief moment upon reaching critical health
		- Has a cooldown of  10 minutes, denoted in the accessory's tooltip
Sigil of Pain Suppression
	- Hardmode Anvil, Freed Dungeon Soul x 15 + Ironskin Potion x 5
	- Drastically increases your defense for a brief moment upon reaching critical health
		- Has a cooldown of  10 minutes, denoted in the accessory's tooltip
Sigil of Retreat
	- Hardmode Anvil, Freed Dungeon Soul x 15 + Recall Potion x 5
	- Teleports you to your spawn point upon reaching critical health
		- Has a cooldown of 10 minutes, denoted in the accessory's tooltip
Slippery Soles
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Frozen Slime Block x2 + Leather x2
	- You move on any block as if it was a Frozen Slime Block
Soles of Fire and Ice
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Lava Waders x1, Ice Skates x1
	- Allows you to walk on water, lava, and thin ice
Star in a Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Shiny Red Balloon x1 + Star in a Bottle x1
	- Increases mana regeneration and jump height. Stacks with other mana regeneration buffs
Star Wisp Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Star in a Balloon + Wisp in a Balloon
	- Increases Mana Regeneration, Jump Height, and glows in the dark
Water Candle in a Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Water Candle x1 + Shiny Red Balloon x1
	- Increases Enemy Spawn Rate, Jump Height, and very faintly glows in the dark
Wisp in a Balloon
	- Tinkerer's Workbench, Shiny Red Balloon x1 + Wisp in a Bottle x1
	- Glows in the dark and increases jump height. Nearly the same light as Wisp in a Bottle
